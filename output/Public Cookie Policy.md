### 2.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on a Visitor's device that store certain information about browsing activity. Cookies help the Company understand how the Site is used, maintain essential functionality, and improve Visitor experience.

### 2.2 Purpose of Cookies

The Company uses Cookies and Tracking Technologies for:

**(a)** operating the Site;
**(b)** remembering Visitor preferences;
**(c)** understanding usage and performance;
**(d)** improving functionality;
**(e)** securing the Site;
**(f)** delivering personalized content or marketing (where applicable and with consent).
