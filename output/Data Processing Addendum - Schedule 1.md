## 7. TYPES OF PERSONAL DATA

### 7.1 Personal Data Categories

**(a)** **Identification Data:** Name, email address, username, employee ID, job title, company name;

**(b)** **Contact Data:** Postal address, telephone number, mobile number;

**(c)** **Account Data:** Login credentials, account settings, preferences;

**(d)** **Usage Data:** IP address, device information, browser type, log data, session data, clickstream data;

**(e)** **Transaction Data:** Payment information (processed by third-party payment processors), billing address, purchase history;

**(f)** **Communication Data:** Email correspondence, support tickets, chat logs;

**(g)** **Technical Data:** API keys, integration settings, configuration data;

**(h)** **Content Data:** Files, documents, images, or other content uploaded by the Client or Authorized Users;

## 11. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

### 11.1 Retention Period

**(a)** The duration of the MSA;  
**(b)** Up to ninety (90) days after termination for transition purposes (if requested by the Client);  
**(c)** Any additional period required by Applicable Law.

### 11.2 Deletion Timeline

Upon termination or expiration of the MSA, the Company shall delete all Personal Data within:

**30 days** (standard, aligned with MSA Section 21.4)

### 11.3 Backup Deletion

Backup copies containing Personal Data shall be overwritten or securely destroyed within:

**90 days** after the deletion date.
