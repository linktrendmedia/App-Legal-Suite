## 3. AUP ↔ SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)

### Relationship

The AUP defines prohibited activities that may trigger suspension under SLA Section 19.1(c).

### Cross-References

- SLA Section 12.1(d): Client misuse or AUP violations are Excluded Events
- SLA Section 15.5(d): Misuse or AUP violations may result in denial of Service Credits

---

## 4. AUP ↔ END USER LICENSE AGREEMENT (EULA)

### Relationship

The AUP is incorporated into the EULA and governs acceptable use of licensed software.

### Harmonization Requirements

- Prohibited activities (AUP Section 2) must align with EULA usage restrictions
- Enforcement actions (AUP Section 8) must align with EULA termination rights
- Export controls (AUP 2.13) must align with EULA export control provisions

---

## 5. AUP ↔ B2C TERMS OF SERVICE

### Relationship

The AUP is incorporated into B2C Terms of Service and governs consumer use of the Services.

### Harmonization Requirements

- Content restrictions (AUP Section 3) must align with B2C ToS content policies
- Account suspension/termination (AUP Section 8) must align with B2C ToS termination provisions
- Children's protections (AUP 2.9) must align with B2C ToS age restrictions

---

## 6. AUP ↔ WEBSITE TERMS OF USE

### Relationship

The AUP is incorporated into Website Terms of Use and governs website visitor behavior.

### Harmonization Requirements

- Prohibited conduct (AUP Section 2) must align with Website Terms prohibited activities
- Content restrictions (AUP Section 3) must align with Website Terms content policies
