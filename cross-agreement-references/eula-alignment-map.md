## 6. EULA ↔ B2C TERMS OF SERVICE

### Relationship

The EULA governs downloadable/installable applications, while B2C ToS governs web-based service access. These are separate agreements for different contexts.

### Shared Concepts (for consistency)

- Age restrictions (13 US/16 EU)
- AI/ML training prohibition
- Biometric data prohibition
- Export controls
- Governing law/jurisdiction
- Limitation of liability (with consumer carve-outs)
