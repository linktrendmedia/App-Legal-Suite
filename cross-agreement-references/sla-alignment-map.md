## 3. SLA ↔ ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY (AUP)

### Relationship

SLA Excluded Events reference violations of the AUP.

### Cross-References

- SLA Section 12.1(d): Client misuse or violations of MSA/AUP
- SLA Section 15.5(d): Misuse or violation of MSA/SLA contributing to issue

---

## 4. SLA ↔ OTHER AGREEMENTS

### Minimal Direct Connection

The SLA is primarily a B2B document that supports the MSA. It has minimal direct connection to:

- B2C Terms of Service
- EULA
- Privacy Policies
- Cookie Policies
- Website Terms of Use

However, all agreements should reference the same:
- Governing law/jurisdiction
- Data protection laws
- Security standards
