## PURPOSE

This document contains harmonized definitions used across the App Legal Suite. All agreements in the suite should use these definitions consistently to ensure clarity and avoid conflicts.

---

## CORE DEFINITIONS

### Affiliate

Means any entity that directly or indirectly controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with a party, where "control" means ownership of more than 50% of the voting securities or the power to direct management.

**Used in:** MSA, DPA, SLA, EULA

---

### Agreement

Means the specific agreement being referenced, together with all schedules, exhibits, and incorporated documents.

**Used in:** All agreements

---

### API

Means the application programming interfaces provided by the Company to enable programmatic access to the Services.

**Used in:** MSA, B2C ToS, EULA, Website Terms

---

### Applicable Law

Includes contract law, data protection law, export regulations, and other relevant legislation specified in the applicable agreement or schedule.

**Used in:** All agreements

---

### Authorized Users

Means employees, contractors, or agents of the Client permitted to use the Services.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA, DPA, EULA

---

### Beta Features

Means experimental or pre-release features, modules, or functionality made available by the Company on a trial basis and designated as "beta," "alpha," "preview," "early access," or similar designation.

**Used in:** MSA, B2C ToS, EULA

---

### Business Day

Means any day other than Saturday, Sunday, or a public holiday in the jurisdiction specified in the applicable agreement.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA, DPA

---

### Change Request

Means a written request by either party to modify the scope, specifications, or delivery schedule of Services or Deliverables.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA

---

### Client

Means the business customer entering into an agreement with the Company.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA, DPA

**Note:** Use "Customer" for B2C agreements.

---

### Client Data

Means Data owned or controlled by the Client.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA, DPA

**Note:** Use "Customer Data" or "User Data" for B2C agreements.

---

### Company

Means the legal entity identified in the applicable agreement as the provider of the Services.

**Used in:** All agreements

---

### Confidential Information

Means all non-public business, technical, or operational information disclosed under the applicable agreement.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA, DPA, EULA

---

### Data

Means all data entered, generated, transmitted, stored, or processed by the Client or Customer through the Services.

**Used in:** All agreements

---

### Deliverables

Means any work product, custom integrations, configurations, or customizations described in an Order Form and delivered by the Company to the Client.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA

---

### Documentation

Means user guides, API documentation, onboarding materials, and other technical documentation provided by the Company.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA, EULA

---

### DPA

Means the Data Processing Addendum incorporated into the applicable agreement by reference.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA, B2C ToS, Privacy Policies

---

### Fees

Means all payments payable under the applicable agreement or any Order Form.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA, EULA, B2C ToS

---

### Force Majeure Event

Means any event beyond the reasonable control of a party, including acts of God, natural disasters, pandemics, epidemics, government orders or restrictions, labor disputes, supply chain disruptions, telecommunications failures, power outages, cloud provider outages, or failures of third-party infrastructure providers.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA

---

### Intellectual Property Rights

Means all patents, copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, database rights, design rights, domain names, moral rights, know-how, and all other intellectual property rights (registered or unregistered) including rights in software, algorithms, machine learning models, and AI-generated outputs.

**Used in:** All agreements

---

### Maintenance Window

Means a scheduled period during which the Company may perform maintenance, updates, or upgrades to the Services, as specified in the SLA.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA

---

### Order Form

Means any ordering document executed between the parties specifying Services, Fees, and commercial terms.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA

---

### Processing

Has the meaning given in the DPA (aligned with GDPR Article 4(2)).

**Used in:** MSA, DPA, Privacy Policies

---

### Security Incident

Means any confirmed incident resulting in unauthorized access to, disclosure of, or loss of Client Data or Customer Data.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA, DPA, Privacy Policies

---

### Services

Means the SaaS products, platforms, features, modules, APIs, and related support provided by the Company, as specified in the applicable agreement.

**Used in:** All agreements

---

### SLA

Means the Service Level Agreement incorporated into the MSA.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA

---

### Subscription Term

Means the period during which the Client or Customer is authorized to use the Services, as specified in the Order Form or applicable agreement.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA, EULA, B2C ToS

---

### Support Services

Means the support and maintenance services described in the SLA.

**Used in:** MSA, SLA

---

## INTERPRETATION RULES

The following interpretation rules apply across all agreements:

**(a)** Headings are for convenience only and do not affect interpretation.

**(b)** Singular includes plural and vice versa.

**(c)** "Including" means "including without limitation."

**(d)** References to laws include amendments, replacements, and successor legislation.

**(e)** References to "writing" or "written" include email.

**(f)** In case of translation discrepancies, the English version prevails.
